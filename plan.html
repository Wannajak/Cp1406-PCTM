Goals

The goal of the new site is to improve the functionality and design to be easier to use as well as a more aesthetically pleasing to look at.

Success Evaluation

The success of the site will be displayed through an increased interest in the website and therefore the orchestra itself. Intrest can be measured through the number of unique visitors to the site per month, as well as backlinking.

Target Audience

1. The target audience for the site is for people with an interest in attending the concerts, as well as for supporters of the orchestra.
2. The site will use visualization and placement of key elements to direct the attention of visitors. Key elements will be the Menu to make navigation easy for users as well as sign up and FAQ sections.
